Chiikawa

Chiikawa is a small, mysterious creature who loves to eat and sleep. With their cute appearance and charming personality, Chiikawa has captured the hearts of fans worldwide. Always curious and friendly, Chiikawa enjoys adventures with friends Hachiware and Usagi.

Friendly Curious Silly

Hachiware

Hachiware is a small cat-like creature who is always full of energy and enthusiasm. Known for being hardworking and loyal, Hachiware is the dependable friend who looks out for everyone. With their blue pattern and cheerful attitude, they bring joy to every adventure with Chiikawa and Usagi.

Energetic Loyal Hardworking

Usagi

The most energeic of the crew, Usagi seems to find the fun. Though the word usagi literally means rabbit in Japanese, and Usagi has bunny-looking ears, it's unclear Usagi's identity. Maybe a rabbit, maybe not. Lively and fearless, Usagi is known for signature yells "Yaha!" and "Ura!". Interestingly, no one knows where Usagi lives.
